ADMISSION RULES

The guidelines for admission to classes from 6th to 9th & 11th in the Maharishi Dayanand Aadarsh Inter College Umrara are as follows:


1.Admission Form

Admission Form can be procured from the office of MDA Inter College in which admission is sought by paying a Fee of Rs. 50/-.


2.Documents to be submitted

     A. Original Birth Certificate along with an attested copy is to be submitted at the time of admission.
     B. Admission will not be granted on the basis of an affidavit in lieu of a birth certificate.
     C. Transfer Certificate is to be submitted when Admission is sought in classes 6 to 10.
     D. A copy of the caste certificate.
